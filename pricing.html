Product Icons

Services:

  • Design an app icon in any style, including Material Design or iOS Flat Design
  • Redesign your current app icon so it will better match current design trends on the platform your product is targeting

Pricing*:

  • For redesigning your current icon - $10+
  • For creating new icon from scratch - $20+
  • For the source file (.psd) you’ll need to pay additional $5

What does the process look like?

After contacting me and setting up all the details required to design an icon, you will be required to pay 50% in advance. I accept both bank transfers and PayPal transactions. Upon receiving the payment, I will begin working on your icon. First few concepts shouldn’t take longer than 4 hours. When they are ready, I will send them to you for feedback. Once the icon is complete, you will be required to pay the remainder of the agreed-upon price. After that, I will send you your product icon in all sizes, as well as a Store (Google Play Store or Apple App Store) version of your icon.


*Student pricing is available

Illustrations

Services:

I also create illustrations that can be used in an app or other medium. (Examples: Store Banners, App Intro screens, headers, etc) Although I prefer creating illustrations in a flat style, I can adapt to any color scheme or design language. Flat style illustrations can be exported to Android Vector drawables, which significantly less space compared to their PNG counterparts. In addition, Vector Drawables, always look crisp on any screen resolution. Prices start with $20 for an illustration. The more illustrations you order, the lower price per illustration will be. Source file(s) will cost additional $5. Student pricing is available.


What does the process look like?

After contacting me and setting up all the details required to design your illustrations, you will be required to pay 50% in advance. I accept both bank transfers and PayPal transactions. Upon receiving the payment, I will begin working on your illustrations. First few concepts shouldn’t take very long. When they are ready, I will send samples to you for feedback. Once the illustrations are complete, you will be required to pay the remainder of the agreed-upon price. After that, I will send you your illustrations in your desired resolutions and file formats.
