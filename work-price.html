Product Icons
What type of work I can do related to icons:
• Design an app icon in any style (most preferably Material Design, commonly used in Android)
• Materialise your current app icon, so it will better match current design trends in Android
• Create an Adaptive icon from scratch, or base it on your current app icon
Prices:
• For Materialising an icon or creating an adaptive icon based on your current icon - Prices start with $10*
• For creating new icon - Prices start with $20*
• For a source file (.psd) you’ll need to pay additional $5
*Prices are negotiable
What does the process look like:
After contacting me and setting up all the details required to design an icon, you will be required to pay in advance 50% of the price (I accept both bank transfers and PayPal transactions).
After getting the money I will start working on your icon. First few concepts shouldn’t take longer than 4 hours. When they will be ready, I will ask you for your feedback.
When the icon will be ready, you will be required to pay the second half of the price. After that I will send you your product icon in all sizes and a Play Store version of your icon.
Illustrations
Work:
I can also offer creating illustrations for your app that can be used in eg. Intro screens, headers, placeholders (for empty lists etc.) or Play Store.
Most preferably I design illustrations in a flat style. I can adapt to any color scheme or design language.
Flat style illustrations can be exported to android drawables, that take much less space compared to PNG drawables, and in addition, they look perfectly crisp on any screen resolution.
Prices start with $20 for an illustration. You can negotiate the price. In addition, the more illustrations you order, the lower price per illustration will be. Source file(s) will cost additional $5.
